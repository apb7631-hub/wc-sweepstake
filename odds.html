How The Points Work
Group Stage

Each team plays 3 matches. Three wins = 9 pts. This alone can be decisive — strong draws with multiple solid teams rack up big group totals.

Knockout Rounds

Every knockout match adds up to 3 pts per win or 1 pt if drawn at 90 mins (goes to pens). The champion plays 7 games — a maximum of 21 pts.

Why Depth Matters

Under this system you don't need the champion to win the sweep. Three teams each scoring 8–10 pts can beat one champion scoring 18 pts if their support is dead weight.

The Danger Draw

Having one elite team + three weak teams is risky. If France wins every game (rare), WOP gets ~17 pts from France + ~3 from the rest. Sly's three solid teams might still outscore that.
